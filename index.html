About

I am currently a researcher at Paris Descartes University and a member of the Machine Learning for Data science (MLDS) group. My main research area is related to Unsupervised Machine Learning and focuses on Dimensionality Reduction, Clustering and Co-clustering. I curruntely develop scalable and effective matrix factorization based approaches for simultaneous data embedding and (co-)clustering.

Research Interests

My main research interest include machine learning, data mining and their application to image classification, text mining, recommender systems and bioinformatics. In particular I focus on developing scalable and effetive unsupervised learning approaches, tailored for high dimensional data, for the tasks of image and text documents (co-)clustering, item recommendation, dimentionality reduction and visualization.

A brief History

I am graduated from USTHB in Algiers where i received my engineer diploma in 2001. Thereafter, i am graduated from Claude Bernard Lyon 1 (UCBL) University where i received my Master degree in Knowledge and Decision in 2010. I obtained my Ph.D diploma in Data Sciences in Paris Descartes University under the supervision of professor Mohamed Nadif in 2016.
